Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SMM AI and our social media growth services

How do I know it's safe?

Completely safe! We never ask for your password or login credentials. We only need your public username/handle to deliver services. Our platform uses industry-standard encryption and we partner with verified SMM providers who follow platform guidelines. Your account security is never compromised.

Where do the likes/followers come from?

We use JustAnotherPanel, a premium SMM panel that provides high-quality followers and engagement from real accounts. These are active users from around the world who engage with content in your niche. We don't use bots or fake accounts - all services come from verified sources that maintain long-term growth.

Do I need to give my password? (No!)

Absolutely not! We NEVER ask for passwords, login credentials, or any sensitive account information. All we need is your public username/handle (like @yourname) and optionally a link to your profile or specific posts. This is the same information that's already public on your social media profiles.

Can I cancel orders?

Orders can be cancelled within the first hour if they haven't started processing. Once your growth campaign begins (usually within 24 hours), orders cannot be cancelled as the services are already being delivered. However, we offer a satisfaction guarantee - if you're not happy with the results, contact our support team for assistance.

How long does it take to see results?

You'll typically see results within 24-48 hours of placing your order. Followers and engagement are delivered gradually over several days to ensure natural-looking growth. Large orders may take 3-7 days to complete fully. You'll receive updates on your order progress through our chat system.

Which platforms do you support?

We support all major social media platforms including Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, and more. Our AI can create custom growth strategies for single platforms or multi-platform campaigns based on your goals and budget.

What's your pricing model?

We use a wallet-based system - no subscriptions or hidden fees. Add funds to your wallet, then our AI creates custom plans based on your budget. Plans typically range from $25-100+ depending on your goals. You only pay for what you use, and unused wallet balance never expires.

What if I'm not satisfied with results?

We offer a satisfaction guarantee on all services. If you're not happy with the quality or delivery of your order, contact our support team within 7 days. We'll work to resolve the issue or provide a partial refund when appropriate. Our goal is your success and satisfaction.

How can I get support?

We offer multiple support channels: Live chat through our AI assistant (available 24/7), email support at support@smm-ai.com, and WhatsApp support for urgent issues. Our team typically responds within 2-6 hours during business hours.
